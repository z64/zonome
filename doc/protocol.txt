zonome protocols

message		parameters	description
---------------------------------------------------------------------
Core
play		x y		plays clip on track x clip y
scene		s		launches scene s
stop		t		stops all clips on track t

Transport
mplay		-		master play
mstop		-		master stop
tempo		x		set tempo to x

Peripheral
function	x y		play function (dummy) clip in fx bank
kaos		x y z		"kaos" pad

* - all subject to change, and may or may not be implemented yet.
